# Architecture Decision Records

This directory contains Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) for the Test01 project.

## What is an ADR?

An ADR documents a significant architectural decision along with its context and consequences. They serve as a historical record of why certain technical choices were made.

## Format

Each ADR follows this structure:

- **Title** — Short noun phrase describing the decision
- **Status** — Proposed, Accepted, Deprecated, or Superseded
- **Context** — The forces at play, including technical, business, and social
- **Decision** — The change we are making
- **Consequences** — What becomes easier or harder as a result
